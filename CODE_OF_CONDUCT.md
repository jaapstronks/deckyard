# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule: **be kind, be constructive, and assume
good intent**.

We welcome contributions from people of all backgrounds. Harassment,
discrimination, and abusive behavior are not tolerated — in issues, pull
requests, discussions, or any other project space.

## Reporting

If something goes wrong, contact the maintainer (see the Maintainers section
in `README.md`). For anything sensitive, use GitHub's private vulnerability
reporting (Security tab) — it reaches the maintainers privately even when the
report isn't strictly a security issue.

Reports are handled confidentially. The maintainers may warn, moderate, or
ban participants who violate this code, at their discretion.
